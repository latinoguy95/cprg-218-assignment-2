Additional Information

Colombian national, I arrived in Canada -specifically in Calgary- in August 2023, after being accepted in the Web Design and Major Development program offered by SAIT. I have a strong bond and sense of belonging to my culture and identify myself as a Latino. I'm also passionate about soccer (I'm a huge Chelsea Football Club supporter), I love listening to Green Day, Sum 41, Kodaline, Simple Plan, Coldplay, and OneRepublic, coffee (to the point that if I don't have it, I'll eventually fall asleep), nature, beer, and sunsets. As an effort of making my social circle bigger, I decided to create this affitionate-photography website and show what I normally do in my free time.

As someone aiming to make their social circle even bigger, I decided to create this afficionate-photography website to show the entire world how someone can turn boredom into something really creative. Exhibiting my afficionate pictures also brings me some satisfaction since I'm discovery myself and showing I can do things spontaneously.
